Our Story

The Soul of braj Federation is a NPO ( Non Profit Organization ) , it comes under section 8, Located in Vrindavan (Mathura). It is born with the pure intention to serve all brijwasi’s as Written in Shrimad bhagvatam “My beloved krishna, I want to become a servant of your servant” Soul of braj Federation has been focusing and trying to make a positive impact on the living standards of the economically poor and disadvantaged people of braj by helping them to provide basic Health care, Water and Sanitation , Basic Education, Hygiene Food and Community Service. Our Collaboration with Prestigious Corporates, individual donors, and well-wishers. Who have helped us to grow and serve Braj Region from all over the world.
