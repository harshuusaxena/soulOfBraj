basic health care

“Soul of Braj” primary goal is to provide primary health care made accessible to individuals in a community at free of cost. We aimed to provide comprehensive and continuous care to the patients. It helps in making the patients available with various social welfare and public health services in initiated by the concerned governing bodies and other organization. A tremendous amount of needless pain and suffering can be eliminated by ensuring that health insurance is universally available. Public health care is characterized by partnership and empowerment of the people that shall permeate as the core strategy in the effective provision of essentials health services that is community based, accessible, acceptable and sustainable at cost which the community and government can afford the concept of primary health care as strategy to reach the goals. We would organize a dedicated team of healthcare professionals offering the best medical services. They provide a coordinated approach to the delivery of health care that ensures that the beneficiaries receive the best care from the right health provider. Best clinical decisions are at the heart of appropriate care, the goal to which our system should aspire. It is much important to know what sort of a patient has a disease than that what sort of disease a patient has. Primary health care is the cornerstone of the health system. It can help us prevent, treat and manage both infectious and non-communicable diseases –bringing the world one step closer to universal health coverage. Health care must be recognized as right, not a privilege. What kind of nation are we when we give tax breaks to billionaires, but we can’t take care of the elderly and children.
