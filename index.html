Vission

Our Foundation is steered by the vision of a healthy community in particular underprivileged backgrounds - can belong and thrive.

Mission

We walk with those who need access to basic facilities like drinking water, sanitation, electricity, housing and drainage arrangement also preserving old heritage . When people get basic facilities then they become more efficient and raise the overall standard of life and well-being.

our values

We are highly committed to serve and value transparency and honesty in our communication, relationship and actions. We hold ourselves accountable the quality and lasting results of our work and for the commitment we make to our partners, donors and each other. We extend the hand of friendship and love specially to those who are underprivileged

ABOUT US

The Soul of braj is an NGO, its comes under section 8 (NPO) headquarter in Vrindavan (Mathura). It is born with the pure intention to serve all brijwasi’s as Written in Shrimad bhagvatam “My beloved krishna,” I want become servant of your servants” Soul of braj has been focusing and trying make a positive impact on the living standards of the economically poor and disadvantaged people of braj by helping them to provide basic health care facilities and sanitation. We are continuously leveraging basic facility like Clean water to drinks and hygiene food to multiply our reach and also preserving old heritage. Our collaboration with SDG Chaupal and Nagrik Foundation , along with the persistent support from prestigious corporates, individual donors, and well-wishers have helped us to grow and serve braj from all over the world.
