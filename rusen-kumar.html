Rusen kumar
Board Member of Soul of Braj Federation

Rusen Kumar is a visionary knowledge entrepreneur, author and thought leader on corporate social responsibility. He is well-known personality in the world of CSR and known for his dedication towards Sustainable Development and Corporate Social Responsibility. He is the founder of India CSR Network indiacsr.in, a leading network for corporate responsibility and sustainability in India. He has established various national and international forums on CSR, sustainability and social issues.
He is among the few leaders who have been actively involved in promoting and spreading awareness on corporate social responsibility and allied issues for almost a decade through numerous interventions. He has been conferred with the prestigious CSR person of the year award, 2017 by Karunya University and giving economy changemaker awards 2018 by Crowdera Foundation for promoting CSR in India. He has also been recognised as a global thought leader on CSR by germany based Future2Day future2day.org.
He brings an understanding of governance, leadership development, social development, human development, and strategic focus by serving for-profit and not-for-profit boards and as an advisor to chief executive officers and executive management members. His leadership accomplishments in social enterprise, planning, and governance range from viable achievements in knowledge forum initiatives to advancement of corporate social responsibility issues in India.

He has travelled the length and breadth of the country to understand and learn from the people and society. He has two books to his credit. Books are – ‘Thoughts, Views and Quotes on CSR’ (English) 2019 and कब पढ़ेंगे आप ‘When Will You Read’ (Hindi) 2019. His more than 6 books are under publication.
